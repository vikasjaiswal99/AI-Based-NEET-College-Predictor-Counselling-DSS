About the Project

NEETPredict AI is a sophisticated decision-support system designed to help medical aspirants navigate the complex landscape of NEET counseling. By analyzing thousands of historical cutoff data points, the system provides accurate predictions for Safe, Target, and Dream colleges.

This project was developed with a focus on data accuracy and user experience, ensuring that every student can make informed decisions about their future career in medicine.
